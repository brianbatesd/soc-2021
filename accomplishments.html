Clearmeadows Park

Vancouver Parks and Recreation completed an improvement project at Clearmeadows Park in the Fishers Landing East Neighborhood. The project installed a new flower and bee-themed playground, new benches, picnic tables and bike racks and resurfaced the basketball court.

Wayfinding Project

Thanks to funding from Lodging Tax grants and other sources, the Community and Economic Development Department completed a downtown wayfinding project, including directional signs, street banners and location maps.
