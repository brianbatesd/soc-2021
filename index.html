Message from the Mayor

Every year during the State of the City Address, we look back on the past year and reflect on what we have achieved. As I thought back on the past year while preparing for this address, it really brought to light just how much our community is growing and changing and what an exciting time it is to live in Vancouver!

As our city grows, so does the demand for City programs and services. Over the past two years, the City has worked with the community to identify the programs and services needed to meet this growing demand in a sustainable and equitable way through the Stronger Vancouver initiative. As of the publication date of this report, City Council was still deliberating on the components to be included in the final Stronger Vancouver package, with completion anticipated in late April. I encourage you to visit A Stronger Vancouver to learn more.

It will be exciting to see our vision for a safe, welcoming, vibrant and prosperous city come to life all throughout our community in the near future through delivery of the full range of services, programs and capital projects included in A Stronger Vancouver.

It is my honor to serve as your mayor, working alongside my fellow City Councilmembers to set the vision, goals and policies that guide decision-making throughout the City organization. The council’s partnership with the City Manager assures we achieve our community’s goals together, and I appreciate the work the City Manager and City employees do every day for the residents of Vancouver.

I look forward to the coming year and continuing to work with all of you on a shared vision for the future of Vancouver—a vibrant, welcoming and prosperous city that offers something for everyone.

Sincerely,

Mayor Anne McEnery-Ogle
